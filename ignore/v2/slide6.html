Key Differences

QLED Advantages

  • Exceptional brightness (4,000+ nits vs 2,400 nits)
  • No burn-in problems
  • Better for bright rooms
  • More affordable
  • Available in larger sizes

OLED Advantages

  • Perfect blacks (infinite contrast)
  • Superior gaming response times
  • Better viewing angles
  • More energy efficient in dark content
  • Thinner display profiles
