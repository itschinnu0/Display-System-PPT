Future Developments

Next Generation QLED Technology

Self-Emissive QLED

  • Quantum dots light up independently (no backlight needed)
  • Each pixel emits light directly, similar to OLED
  • Faster response times with perfect pixel control
  • Better contrast and viewing angles
  • Challenge: Lifetime and manufacturing issues

MicroLED Integration

  • Quantum dots combined with MicroLED technology
  • Self-lighting pixels without degradation
  • Modular, flexible screen designs
  • Enhanced brightness and power efficiency
  • Longer lifespans than current display technologies

QLED technology continues evolving toward display systems that combine the best of all worlds: QLED's brightness and color accuracy, OLED's contrast levels, and MicroLED's durability and efficiency.
