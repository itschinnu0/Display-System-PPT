Quantum Confinement Effect

The key science behind QLED is remarkably simple:

The size of quantum dots precisely determines what color they produce:

Blue Light
Smaller Dots (2-4nm)
Green Light
Medium Dots (4-6nm)
Red Light
Larger Dots (6-10nm)

This precision allows manufacturers to create any color by controlling the quantum dot size

Enabling 100% color volume and unprecedented color accuracy
