Key Advantages of QLED

Exceptional Brightness

QLED displays reach 2,000 to 4,000+ nits of brightness, perfect for bright rooms and HDR content viewing

Superior Color Quality

Covers nearly 100% of Rec. 2020 color gamut with pure, saturated colors and minimal mixing

No Burn-in Risk

Unlike OLED screens, QLED displays are immune to static image burn-in problems

Longer Lifespan

Durable inorganic materials maintain stable picture quality over time with extended operational life

Energy Efficiency

30-40% more power efficient than traditional displays, especially when displaying bright content
