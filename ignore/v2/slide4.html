Key Principles of QLED Technology

  • Blue LED Backlight: Provides the initial light source.
  • Quantum Dot Enhancement Film: Contains quantum dots that convert blue light to red and green.
  • LCD Panel: Controls which pixels light up (light transmission).
  • Color Filters: Uses traditional RGB filters for final color production.
