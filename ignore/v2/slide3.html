History & Nobel Prize Discovery

Alexei Ekimov
1981
First discovered quantum dots while studying colored crystals in glass
Alexander Efros
1982
Explained the science behind how quantum dots work
Louis Brus
1983
Created quantum dots in liquid form at Bell Labs
2023 Nobel Prize in Chemistry
All three scientists won the 2023 Nobel Prize in Chemistry for their groundbreaking work in quantum dots, which led to QLED technology
2001
Samsung started researching quantum dots for displays
2014
Samsung created cadmium-free quantum dots, solving environmental safety problems
2015
Samsung launched the first commercial QLED TVs (SUHD series)
